NutriPlays

Product Thinking

NutriPlays is an interactive educational web application that helps young primary school children learn healthy eating by building their own lunchbox through colour-based food grouping and playful interaction. The project connects nutrition learning with New Zealand health and physical education goals, while demonstrating product thinking, target-user research, accessibility-aware UI design, competitor analysis, educational theory mapping, and user testing for an age-appropriate digital learning experience.
