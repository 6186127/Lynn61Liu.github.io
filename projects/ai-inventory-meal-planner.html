AI-Enabled Inventory & Meal Planning Application

MERN

A full-stack AI-enabled refrigerator inventory and meal planning application that helps users manage food items, generate weekly lunchbox meal plans based on available ingredients, compare grocery prices from Countdown and Pak'nSave, receive recipe suggestions, and build shopping lists. The project demonstrates how AI, API integration, and user-centred design can reduce food waste, support budget-aware grocery planning, and improve everyday household decision-making.
