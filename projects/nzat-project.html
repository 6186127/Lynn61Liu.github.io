NZAT Workshop Management System

Full-Stack Project

A full-stack workshop management platform built from scratch to digitise and automate paper-based workshop operations. The system centralises vehicle records, customer information, job tracking, WOF service records, work logs, invoices, purchase orders, and follow-up tasks, while integrating Excel, email, Xero, and internal data sources. By replacing fragmented manual processes with a centralised digital workflow, the project helps reduce duplicate data entry, improve operational efficiency, increase process traceability, minimise errors, and create a scalable foundation for future reporting, automation, and business growth.
